TITLE:
Old Age Home (Sahara)

==================================================

BENEFITS:
- Construction and operation of old age home "Sahara"These old age homes also include housing, clothes, food, medical facilities etc.
- Facilities will be available for qualitative improvement in the standard of living, cooperation and proper care of destitute and poor old people of the state.

--------------------------------------------------

ELIGIBILITY:
- Bihar Old Age Home (Sahara)Shelter Homes Social Welfare Vrihad Sahayata Applicants should come under the below poverty line ( BPL) category (or)Annual Income should be below Rs.
- 60,000/- Bihar Old Age Home (Sahara)Shelter Homes Social Welfare Vrihad Sahayata

--------------------------------------------------

DOCUMENTS:
- Age Proof.
- Income proof document.
- BPL Card (If applicable).
- Two passport-size photographs.

--------------------------------------------------

APPLICATION PROCESS:
Offline Applicants can apply through the application in plain paper to the District Social Security Cell.The committee constituted under the chairmanship of the District Magistrate will approve the proposal.

--------------------------------------------------

DETAILS:
Under this scheme construction and operation of the old age home "Sahara" will be included in which destitute Housing, clothes, food, medical facilities, etc. are made available for qualitative improvement in the standard of living of poor and poor elders, for cooperation and proper care.
