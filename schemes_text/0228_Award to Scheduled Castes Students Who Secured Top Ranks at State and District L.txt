TITLE:
Award to Scheduled Castes Students Who Secured Top Ranks at State and District Level in Standard X & XII (Sahuji Maharaj Assistance)

==================================================

BENEFITS:
- Award/Cash Prizes

--------------------------------------------------

ELIGIBILITY:
- Gujarat Award to Scheduled Castes Students Who Secured Top Ranks at State and District Level in Standard X & XII (Sahuji Maharaj Assistance)Award Cash Prize SCStudent Top Rank The students should belong to the Scheduled Castes Category of Gujarat.
- The students who have Secured Top Ranks at the State and District Levels in Gujarat in Standard X & Standard XII across all streams are eligible for cash prizes.
- There is no income limit.
- Gujarat Award to Scheduled Castes Students Who Secured Top Ranks at State and District Level in Standard X & XII (Sahuji Maharaj Assistance)Award Cash Prize SCStudent Top Rank

--------------------------------------------------

DOCUMENTS:
- Passport size photo Identity proof i.e.
- Aadhaar Card Caste Certificate from the Competent Authorities Educational certificate/Marksheet Identity Card (School)Any other documents as required

--------------------------------------------------

APPLICATION PROCESS:
Offline Under this scheme, the list of students is obtained from the Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Board and the award is given to the selected students accordingly.

--------------------------------------------------

DETAILS:
The scheme "Award to Scheduled Castes Students Who Secured Top Ranks at State and District Level in Standard X & XII" is initiated by the Director Scheduled Caste Welfare, Department of Social Justice & Empowerment, Government of Gujarat. Under the scheme, the Scheduled Castes Category students who have Secured Top Ranks at the State and District Levels in Gujarat in Standard X & XII are given cash prizes. Cash prizes are awarded to the first three students who secure the highest ranks in both Standard X and Standard XII across all streams. at State Level:Sl.
