TITLE:
Old Age Pension-West Bengal

==================================================

BENEFITS:
- Financial Assistance

--------------------------------------------------

ELIGIBILITY:
- West Bengal Old Age Pension-West Bengal Financial Assistance Pension Social Welfare Applicant must be resident of West Bengal State.
- Applicant must be 60 years old or above, but in the case of physically and mentally handicapped individuals, the age limit for eligibility is 55 years.
- The person should not have any relation capable of providing financial support.
- Here "relation" means and includes-Son, Grand Son.
- Husband, Wife.
- Unmarried daughter.
- Parents.
- The income of the person should not exceed ₹ 1,000/- per month.
- The applicant should not be a recipient of any other Government pension.
- Residency Duration The disabled person should have been a resident of the state of West Bengal for at least 10 years at the time of applying for the pension. (OR)Exception: The State Government may relax the residency condition in cases where undue hardship is caused.
- West Bengal Old Age Pension-West Bengal Financial Assistance Pension Social Welfare

--------------------------------------------------

DOCUMENTS:
- Copy of Aadhaar self-attested.
- Copy of Ration Card.
- Copy of Voter Id.
- Copy of Disability Certificate.
- Copy of Income Certificate.
- Copy of Husband's Death Certificate.
- Copy of Bank Pass Book.
- Nomination Form (In case of death).
- Passport Size photograph.

--------------------------------------------------

APPLICATION PROCESS:
Offline Application Form(Form-P) for Pension may be obtained free of cost from following offices namely:Office of the Block Development Officer or the Executive Officer of the concerned Panchayat Samity in case of the applicant resides in the rural areas under the said Panchayat Samity.Office of the Sub-Divisional Officer in case of the applicant resides in Municipal / Notified areas outside the areas of Kolkata Municipal Corporation.Office of the Controller of Vagrancy, West Bengal situated at Purta Bhawan, Bidhan Nagar, Kolkata-700091, in case of the applicant resides within the areas under Kolkata Municipal Corporation.Required information for application form Person
