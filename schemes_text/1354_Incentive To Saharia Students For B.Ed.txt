DETAILS:
The basic objective of the scheme is to provide financial assistance to the students of the Saharia region for B.Ed training so that the tribal students of the Saharia region can keep their participation in the government service. In this scheme, hostel fees, scholarships (stipends), tuition fees, and stationery are given to these students.
