TITLE:
Lakshmi Bai Samajik Suraksha Pension Yojana

==================================================

BENEFITS:
- Financial Assistance of Rs.
- 300/- per month.

--------------------------------------------------

ELIGIBILITY:
- Bihar Lakshmi Bai Samajik Suraksha Pension Yojana Empowerment Financial Assitance Widow Pension Women Welfare Widow woman must be a resident of Bihar.
- The woman should be a widow.
- The woman should be living below the poverty line.
- The annual income of the woman's family should be Rs.
- 60,000/- or less.
- The age of the woman should be 18 years or more.
- Bihar Lakshmi Bai Samajik Suraksha Pension Yojana Empowerment Financial Assitance Widow Pension Women Welfare

--------------------------------------------------

DOCUMENTS:
- Female Aadhaar card.
- Address proof.
- BPL ration card.
- Age certificate.
- Income certificate.
- Husband's death certificate.
- Identity card.
- Bank account statement.
- Mobile number.
- Email id.
- Passport-size photo.
