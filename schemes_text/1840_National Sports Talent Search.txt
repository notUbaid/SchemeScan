TITLE:
National Sports Talent Search

==================================================

BENEFITS:
- The program will help in finding athletes with natural abilities like anthropometric, physical, and physiological capacities without any anatomical defects among children for admission in Class IV to Class VI.
- The program will support the growth of Indian sports, especially rural sports.
- Sports schools at the district, regional, and national levels as well as national and international sports academies will foster the development of athletes' sporting potential and talent.
- As a result of this, the number of athletes in the nation will increase.
