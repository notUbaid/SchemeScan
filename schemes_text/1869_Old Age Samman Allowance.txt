TITLE:
Old Age Samman Allowance

==================================================

BENEFITS:
- Provides social security to elderly people who are unable to sustain themselves with their own resources.
- It aims to ensure the benefits of old age allowance to the needy and poorer sections of the society.
- The age of eligibility has been reduced from 65 to 60 years.
- The rates of the allowance have been increased over the years.
- The pension is being disbursed through Panchayati Raj Institution (PRI) from February 2006. .

--------------------------------------------------

APPLICATION PROCESS:
Online Offline Step 1: Visit the website https://pension.socialjusticehry.gov.in/Online_Application Step 2: Download the pension form by clicking on "Click here to download pension form" or take a printout of the application form from the website and fill in all the necessary details mentioned in the form.Step 3: Get the filled form signed by the authorized authority such as Sarpanch/MC/Numberdar.Step 4: Scan the fully filled and signed application form (File size should be up to 1 MB in PDF format) and also scan the personal identity documents such as Aadhaar card, Haryana residence certificate, etc. before applying online.Step 5: Create a login ID on the Saral website. After successfully logging in, click on "Apply for Services."Step 6: Search for "Citizen Registration" and fill in the Citizen Registration form with the required parameters to get the CIDR ID.Step 7: After CIDR registration, search for "Department of Social Justice & Empowerment" and select the service "Old Age Samman Allowance."Step 8: To fill in the pension form, enter the CIDR ID, name of the applicant, and Aadhaar number and click on "Search."Step 9: Fill in the pension form online and upload all necessary scanned documents like the pension form, Haryana residence certificate, etc.Step 10: Once the application is submitted online, you will receive a Saral ID for future reference. Take a printout of the online filled form.Step 11: Submit the filled application form along with necessary documents at block or DSWO offices.

DETAILS:
The Old Age Samman Allowance Scheme is a social security scheme implemented by the government of Haryana, India, to provide financial assistance to elderly persons who are unable to sustain themselves with their own resources. Initially started as the Old Age Pension Scheme in 1964, it has undergone various modifications and enhancements and was renamed the Old Age Samman Allowance Scheme in 1991. The scheme aims to ensure the benefits of old age allowance to the needy and poorer sections of society, such as agricultural labourers, rural artisans, SCs/BCs, and small/marginal farmers. The scheme provides a monthly pension to eligible persons who are 60 years or above, and whose combined income from all sources, including that of their spouse, does not exceed Rs. 2,00,000/- per annum. The pension amount has been increased over time and currently stands at Rs. 2500 per month since April 2021. The scheme is implemented through the Zila Parishads, and the pension is disbursed through the Panchayati Raj Institutions. Income Certificate Domicile Certificate Photograph of the applicant
