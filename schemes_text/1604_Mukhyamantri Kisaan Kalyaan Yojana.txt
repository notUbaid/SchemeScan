ELIGIBILITY:
- Madhya Pradesh Mukhyamantri Kisaan Kalyaan Yojana Applicants must be a permanent resident of Madhya Pradesh.
- Applicants must be a farmer.
- The applicant should be registered under PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana.
- Applicant should have cultivable land in which they are doing farming.

DETAILS:
Mukhyamantri Kisan Kalyan Yojana has been started by the Government of Madhya Pradesh for all the poor farmers of MP. The objective of this important scheme is to improve the economic condition of the farmers and provide them sufficient amount for farming. Many times farmers need money to maintain farming, for which Kisan Kalyan Yojana has been started.Chief Minister Kisan Kalyan Yojana is to fulfill the concept of making agriculture a profitable business, to use advanced technology for agriculture, to fulfill the objective of increasing the income of farmers and to make the farmers of the state self-reliant.
