TITLE:
Samagra Samajik Suraksha Parityakta Pension Yojana

==================================================

BENEFITS:
- Pension Pension : ₹ 600/- per month Pension

--------------------------------------------------

ELIGIBILITY:
- Madhya Pradesh Samagra Samajik Suraksha Parityakta Pension Yojana BPLEmpowerment Financial The applicant should be a native of Madhya Pradesh.
- The Abandoned women aged between 18 to 59 years.
- The applicant should be living below the poverty line.
- The applicant name should be mentioned on the Samagr portal.
- Madhya Pradesh Samagra Samajik Suraksha Parityakta Pension Yojana BPLEmpowerment Financial

--------------------------------------------------

DOCUMENTS:
- Three photos of yourself B.
- L. card Certificate to confirm agecertificate of abandonment9-digit composite ID

--------------------------------------------------

APPLICATION PROCESS:
Online Offline Step 1 Go to the Samajik Suraksha portal.Step 2: Click on Samajik Suraksha pension avm arthik sahayata yojanaen.Step 3: Now click on the left on your screen Pension Yojana hetu online aavedan.Step 4: Fill in the required details and submit.

--------------------------------------------------

DETAILS:
Samagra Samajik Suraksha Parityakta Pension Yojana under the Department of Social Justice government of Madhya Pradesh provides financial benefits to Be from a family living below the poverty line. to fulfill the basic needs of life.
