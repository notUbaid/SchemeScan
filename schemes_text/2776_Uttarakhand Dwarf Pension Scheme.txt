DETAILS:
Dwarf Pension Scheme is started by the state Government of Uttarakhand for Dwarf people. The dwarf person has to face difficulty in living his life in a social environment. Sometimes, it's not easy for getting employment for Dwarf people. The main purpose of the scheme is to provide pensions and connect the dwarf people of the Uttarakhand state to the mainstream of society. The main aim of this scheme is to provide social security to the Dwarf people of the state who are unable to sustain themselves from their own resources. So, this scheme will provide a better livelihood and make their life easy. In this scheme, dwarf below 4 feet can eligible for getting benefits of the pension.
