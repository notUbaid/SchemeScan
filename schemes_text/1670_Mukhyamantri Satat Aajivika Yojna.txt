TITLE:
Mukhyamantri Satat Aajivika Yojna

==================================================

BENEFITS:
- Training programs will be organized for eligible women.
- Selected beneficiaries will get a scholarship of Rs 1,000/- during the training period.
- After the completion of the training, the beneficiaries will get a grant of up to Rs 50,000/-.
- Under The Scheme ?
- Training program scholarship and financial assistance.
- Who Can Get The Of The Scheme ?
- The beneficiary should be from the dependent, widow, and weak section and full fill the eligibility criteria.
- What is the minimum and maximum age eligibility criteria ?
- The age of the beneficiary should be between 18 years to 50 years.
- What Scholarship amount is provided during the training period ?
- Selected beneficiaries will get a scholarship of Rs 1,000/- during the training period.
- What Grant Amount is Provided after the training programme ?
- The beneficiaries will get a grant of up to Rs 50,000/-.
- May other state person apply for the scheme ?
- The beneficiary should be a native of Uttarakhand state.
- Can Beneficiaries of other similar scheme get the benefits of this scheme ?
- The beneficiary should not be benefited from similar business from any other scheme.
- How To Apply For The Scheme ?
- Applicants can get the application form from the Women's Empowerment and Child Development Office.
- Fill out the application form carefully and submit it to the office along with all the necessary documents.
- The submitted application will be verified by the Women Empowerment and Child Development Department.
- After the clean verification, the applicant can avail the benefits of the scheme.
- What Documents Are Needed ?
- Basic address proof.
- Aadhar card.
- Voter ID card.
- Ration magazine.
- Death Certificate / Divorce Certificate of Husband.
- Passport- size photo.

--------------------------------------------------

ELIGIBILITY:
- Uttarakhand Mukhyamantri Satat Aajivika Yojna The beneficiary should be from the dependent, widow, and weak section.
- The age of the beneficiary should be between 18 years to 50 years.
- The beneficiary should be a native of Uttarakhand state.
- The beneficiary should not be benefited from similar business from any other scheme.
- Uttarakhand Mukhyamantri Satat Aajivika Yojna

DETAILS:
To provide social and economic empowerment to destitute, widow, and women of weaker sections.Providing the basis of economic security to the destitute and widowed women.Establishment of women specific infrastructure.To move towards self-reliance by providing self-employment opportunities to women of all weaker sections.Providing economic security and stability to women to lead a dignified life in old age.
