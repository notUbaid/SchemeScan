TITLE:
Marriage Assistance

==================================================

BENEFITS:
- A sum of Rs.
- 10,000/- for both male and female is given as Marriage Assistance to employees or their Son/Daughter who legally attain the age of marriage.

--------------------------------------------------

ELIGIBILITY:
- Tamil Nadu Marriage Assistance Daughter Employees Marriage Son Tamil Nadu Should have contributed to Labour Welfare Fund.
- Employee's monthly salary not exceeding Rs.
- 25,000/- (Basic pay + DA).
- Tamil Nadu Marriage Assistance Daughter Employees Marriage Son Tamil Nadu

--------------------------------------------------

DOCUMENTS:
- Marriage Invitation Original.
- School Transfer Certificate(TC) for both Bride and Groom attested by Government gazette officers The Receipt of the Marriage Hall / Temple / Register Marriage Certificate Xerox with Government gazette officers attestation.
- Aadhar card and Ration card Xerox with Government gazette officers attestation.
- Salary slip of the Employee.
- Labour Welfare Fund contribution Receipt Xerox.
- Name list of the paid Labour Welfare Fund.
- Bank passbook front page - 2 copies.

--------------------------------------------------

APPLICATION PROCESS:
Offline Applicants who require the application form in person or self-addressed Rs.- Postage It can be obtained through the enclosed envelope.Address to apply.- Secretary, Tamil Nadu Professional Welfare Board, PO Box No. 718, T.M.S. Campus, Thenampet, Chennai-600 006.

--------------------------------------------------

DETAILS:
The Government of Tamil Nadu introduced "Marriage Assistance" with a sum of Rs.10,000/- for both male and female is given as Marriage Assistance to employees or their Son/Daughter who legally attain the age of marriage.
