- Rajasthan Devnarayan Pre-Matric Scholarship Scheme Education Financial Assistance Scholarship Student The students should be a native of Rajasthan state.

- Banjara, Baladiya, Labana. (ii).
- Gadia-Lohar, Gadolia.(iii).
- Gujar, Gurjar. (iv).
- Raika, Raibari (Debasi).(v).
