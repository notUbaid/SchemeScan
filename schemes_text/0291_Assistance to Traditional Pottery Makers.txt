TITLE:
Assistance to Traditional Pottery Makers

==================================================

BENEFITS:
- The financial assistance of ₹25,000/- will be provided to the Traditional Pottery Makers for upgrading the occupation and purchasing machinery.

--------------------------------------------------

ELIGIBILITY:
- Kerala Assistance to Traditional Pottery Makers Financial Assistance OBCPottery Pottery Worker 1.
- The applicant should be a permanent resident of the Kerala State.
- The applicant should belong to the State ward Community of Kerala State.
- The applicant should be currently engaged in a traditional pottery-making occupation.
- The annual family income of the applicant should be ₹1,00,000/-.
- Kerala Assistance to Traditional Pottery Makers Financial Assistance OBCPottery Pottery Worker

DETAILS:
Certain communities among the Other ward Classes are engaged in traditional occupations including pottery. Traditional pottery workers are following conventional methods for manufacturing products and face tough competition in the market. In this circumstance, this traditional industry has to be revived by imparting training to pottery workers on modern methods/techniques of production and also by providing financial assistance to mechanize and modernize the sector.In view of the above, the scheme "Assistance to Traditional Pottery Makers" is launched by the word Classes Development Department, Government of Kerala. Under the scheme, financial assistance will be provided to Traditional Pottery makers belonging to the state OBC category for the purchase of modern machinery for job modernization.
