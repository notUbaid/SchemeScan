TITLE:
Mukhyamantri Bayan Jyoti Yojana

==================================================

BENEFITS:
- The government is going to distribute 20 watts of tube lights free of cost, for this you will not have to pay any money.
- The second advantage of this Bayana Jyoti Yojana is that the workers will also be given ₹ 17500 free of cost to buy inverters under this scheme, which the Odisha government will give money for.
- The third advantage of this Bayana Jyoti Yojana is that a fan of 35 watts will be given to the beneficiary officers, whose money will be given by the Orissa government itself and all the beneficiaries will get a fan of 35 watts.
- 4th Benefit: Its last and most important benefit is that under the Ballia scheme, they will be given a loan of up to ₹ 5000, in which no interest rate will be charged to them, that is, there will be no improvement in it.

--------------------------------------------------

ELIGIBILITY:
- Odisha Mukhyamantri Bayan Jyoti Yojana Bayan Jyoti Mukhyamantri He/She must be a bonafide weaver / full-time ancillary worker. if.
- He/She is a permanent resident/ domicile of Odisha.
- The family income from all sources does not exceed Rs.
- 3, 00,000k per annum.
- At least 50% income of the family should be from weaving activities.
- He/She must not have availed such type of benefits earlier from the State Government / Central Government / any Government Agency.
- He or she should purchase the lighting unit from a GST-registered dealer with valid TIN.
- Where more than one member of a family is found eligible, only one of them will be entitled to benefit under this scheme Odisha Mukhyamantri Bayan Jyoti Yojana Bayan Jyoti Mukhyamantri

--------------------------------------------------

DOCUMENTS:
- List of documents to be submitted along with the application form.
- Copy of recent passport-size color photograph.
- Self-attested copy of the Weavers Identity Card or certificate that he is a bonafide weaver or ancillary worker.
- Self-attested copy of AADHAR card.
- Self-attested Xerox copy of the 1st page of the individual Savings Bank Pass Bock showing photograph, full name, name of father/spouse, address, bank, branch, account number, IFSC and MICR code details into which the assistance will be deposited.
- Account must be in the name of the applicant as submitted in the application form.
- Account must be opened in a Bank with a core banking facility to enable the digital transfer of assistance.
- Certificate regarding total annual family income from the concerned Tahasildar.
- An affidavit that he/she is not in receipt of similar assistance from either the State or Central Government.
- An undertaking to the effect that the beneficiary will not sell off the lighting unit within three years from the date of purchase.
