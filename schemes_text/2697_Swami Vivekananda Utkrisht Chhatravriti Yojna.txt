TITLE:
Swami Vivekananda Utkrisht Chhatravriti Yojna

==================================================

BENEFITS:
- A scholarship of ₹ 18,000/- . ?
- The guidelines do not mention any provision for compensation in the event of delayed disbursement of scheme benefits.
- Applicants should note that compensation for delays is not part of the scheme's framework.
- Is It Mandatory to Have a Bank Account for This Scheme?
- Yes, possession of a valid bank account is a mandatory requirement for participation in this scheme.
- All scheme benefits are disbursed directly to the beneficiary's registered bank account.
- Are there any specific provisions for persons with disabilities under this scheme?
- No provisions for persons with disabilities are mentioned in the guidelines of this scheme.

--------------------------------------------------

ELIGIBILITY:
- Himachal Pradesh Swami Vivekananda Utkrisht Chhatravriti Yojna Education Financial Assistance Scholarship School Student 1.
- The student should be a resident of Himachal Pradesh.
- The applicant should be a Student.
- The applicant should have passed Class 10th.
- The applicant should have come in merit (in top 2000).
- The applicant should be from the general category.
- The applicant should be enrolled in Class 11th or any professional/technical course in a recognized institute.
- Himachal Pradesh Swami Vivekananda Utkrisht Chhatravriti Yojna Education Financial Assistance Scholarship School Student

--------------------------------------------------

DOCUMENTS:
- Passport Size Photo.
- Aadhaar Card(UID / EID No).
- Himachal Bonafide Certificate.
- Previous Year(s) Result Card(s) from Matric onwards.
- Latest Bank Statement of the Students Bank Account.
- General Category Certificate (Issued by an officer not below the rank of Tehsildar)(DDHE / Head of the Institute is directed to check this Certificate strictly, during verification).

--------------------------------------------------

APPLICATION PROCESS:
Online Step 1: Go to the Official Website of the National Scholarship Portal. and click "New Registration". Guidelines for Registration will appear. Scroll to the bottom. Read carefully the undertaking. Accept the Terms. Click "Continue".Step 2: A Registration Form will appear. The fields marked as * are mandatory. Fill in the details and click "Register". Your Application ID and password will be displayed. The same will also be sent as an SMS to your registered mobile number. Step 3: Go to https://scholarships.gov.in/fresh/newstd Regfrm Instruction Click on "Login to Apply". Enter your Application ID and password. Type the Captcha and click "Login". On the next screen, provide the OTP received on your registered mobile number. You will be directed to the Password Reset screen. Create a new password and confirm. Click "Submit". You will be directed to the "Applicant's Dashboard".Step 4: On the left pane, click "Application Form". The fields marked as * are compulsory. Fill in the details and upload the documents. You can either click on "Save as Draft" to complete the application later. Else, click "Final Submit" to apply.NOTE: All the applicants are advised to fill the form correctly, no changes can be made once submitted. The applicants are also advised to fill in only one application as multiple applications will cancel the form.

--------------------------------------------------

DETAILS:
The scheme "Swami Vivekananda Utkrisht Chhatravriti Yojna" is a Scholarship Scheme by the Department of Education, Government of Himachal Pradesh. The scheme aims to support meritorious students in their pursuit of higher education. Under this scheme, an amount of ₹ 18,000/- will be given to the eligible students per year for two successive years after matric. Every year, 2000 scholarships are awarded to excellent students from the general category.
