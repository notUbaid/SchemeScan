- Incentive to Indian Coaches:1.
- The amount of the cash award payable to the coaches will be as under : In case of Individual events, award money to be given to the set of coaches will be 50% of the award money payable to the sportsperson (s) whom the set of coaches had coached.
- In case of team events, award money given to the set of coaches of the medal winning team will be the same as to the set of coaches as stated above for similar medal.
- The set of coaches could comprise of 1 or more coaches.
- In case, the set contains more than one coach, the said award money will be distributed among all the coaches in the set, equally.
- The amount of cash award payable to coaches of medal winners in individual events will be distributed among the following types of coaches in the ratio mentioned against each category : Coach at grass root level who initially spotted the talent and groomed: 30% of the amount payable to the coaches.
- Coach at the developmental level who further groomed and nurtured the medal winning sportspersons which would also include personal coach : 30% of the amount payable to the coaches.
- Coach at the elite level who have been part of the coaching camps or imparted training to the medal winner for at least 180 days in the preceding two year period before the event : 40% of the amount payable to coach(es).
- Names for coach at grassroot level and coach at the developmental level will be as recommended by the medal winning sportsperson.
- However, spouse, father, mother, brother and sister of the sportspersons will not be eligible for cash award in these cases.
- For the Coach(es) at the elite level who have been part of the coaching camps or imparted training to the medal winner for at least 180 days in the preceding two year period before the event, the set of coaches could comprise of 1 or more coaches.
- In case, the set contains more than one coach, the said award money will be distributed among all the coaches in the set, equally.
- The Cash Awards for the Coach(es) at the elite level will be given regardless of whether a coach is a Chief coach or Assistant Coach subject to the condition that the coach should have been associated with the training of the athlete/team on a regular basic for at least a minimum period of 180 days, i.e., each coach in the set of coaches should have been associated with the medal winning sportsperson / team for at least 180 days on a regular basis.
- Sports Authority of India (SAI) will devise a mechanism and build up a database about the grassroot coaches, developmental coaches including personal coaches for verifying the recommendations made by the medal winning sportsperson for giving cash award.
- If in an event, more than one medal is won by different individual sportspersons trained by the same coach, the coach will get the cash award only for the highest medal.
- If there is any doubt/dispute about the names of coaches recommended for cash award, the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports will have the final say in the matter after due deliberations.

APPLICATION PROCESS:
Offline Online Step 1: Take print of the prescribed proforma of the application form (Refer Page number 8 and 9 of the scheme guidelines) and the Mandate Form (Refer Page number 10 of the scheme guidelines) available at: https://dbtyas-sports.gov.in/assets/annexure/cash_award.pdf Step 2: The moment an athlete wins a competition making him/her eligible for a cash award, the Program Officer in charge at SAI shall intimate the Ministry via electronic media followed by a written letter. Step 3: After the issue of sanction by the Ministry, SAI shall credit the cash award the same day to the bank account of the medal winner through the DBT platform. For this purpose, SAI shall draw an advance from the Ministry, which shall be recouped from time to time, and maintained with it.Note 1: For coaches, the applications will be forwarded by National Sports Federations (NSFs) to the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports and the Ministry will get the achievements verified from SAI before making the payment of cash award to coach(s).Note 2: For de-recognized or suspended NSF, the sportsperson/coach will apply directly to Sports Authority of India (SAI).

DETAILS:
The "Scheme of Cash Awards to Medal Winners in International Sports Events and Their Coaches" was introduced in the year 1986 by the Department of Sports, Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports. The objective of this scheme is to incentivize the achievements of outstanding sportspersons, encourage and motivate them for higher achievements and to act as inspiring role models for attracting the younger generation to take up sports.The Awards will be given in the following disciplines:Sports disciplines in Olympic Games/ Asian Games/ Commonwealth Games Chess Billiards & Snooker (Bank Account Number, Bank name, Branch name, Branch Address, Telephone No. and e-mail, Branch's IFS Code, Type of bank account MICR Code) (Bank Account Number, Bank name, Branch name, Branch Address, Telephone No. and e-mail, Branch's IFS Code, Type of bank account MICR Code)Is There Any Guideline Regarding The Enhancement In The Amount Of The Award Money?The amount of award money may be increased once in four years, by the Minister-in-charge of Youth Affairs & Sports on the recommendations of a Committee chaired by the Secretary (Sports), which will, inter alia, include the Financial Adviser of the Ministry.What If In An Event, More Than One Medal Is Won By Different Individual Sportspersons Trained By The Same Coach?If in an event, more than one medal is won by different individual sportspersons trained by the same coach, the coach will get the cash award only for the highest medal. What If There Is Any Doubt/Dispute About The Names Of Coaches Recommended For Cash Awards?If there is any doubt/dispute about the names of coaches recommended for a cash award, the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports will have the final say in the matter after due deliberations.What Will Be The Incentive Of The Coaches In Case Of Individual Event? In case of Individual events, award money to be given to the set of coaches will be 50% of the award money payable to the sportsperson (s) whom the set of coaches had coached. What If In An International Sports Event, More Than One Medal Is Won By A Sportsperson (In Individual Or Team Category)?If in an international sports event, more than one medal is won by a sportsperson (in individual or team category), he/she will be eligible for cash award only for the highest medal won by him/her in that event.Which Are The Eligible International Tournaments For Billiards And Snooker?The cash award in Billiards and Snooker will be given to the medal winners and their coaches for winning medals in IBSF World Billiards Championship(150 up and Long up format), ISBF World Snooker Championship (both World championships held annually) and ACBS Asian Billiards Championship, ACBS Asian Snooker Championship (both Asian Championships held annually) events held annually.
