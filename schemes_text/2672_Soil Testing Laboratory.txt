TITLE:
Soil Testing Laboratory

==================================================

BENEFITS:
- To provide soil testing services for farmers free of cost.
- Soil Health Card (SHC).

--------------------------------------------------

ELIGIBILITY:
- Meghalaya Soil Testing Laboratory Agriculture Farmers Soil Health Bonafide farmers engaged in Agriculture Meghalaya Soil Testing Laboratory Agriculture Farmers Soil Health

--------------------------------------------------

DOCUMENTS:
- Farmer's Photo.
- Verification Form by the Agriculture Development Officer of a CD Block/Agriculture Circle.
- Bank

--------------------------------------------------

APPLICATION PROCESS:
Offline Bonafide farmers can apply in plain paper through the nearest Agriculture Development Officer of a CD Block/Agriculture Circle.

--------------------------------------------------

DETAILS:
Description Soil Testing Lab - For analysing soil samples preceding the application of fertilizer in the correct dosage in farmers' field.Objectives The analysis is free of cost of soil samples preceding application of fertilizers/ liming etc. for farmers along with distribution of Soil Health Card (SHC).Mode of Selection of Beneficiary(s)Bonafide farmers have sufficient available cultivable land on a first come first served basis.
