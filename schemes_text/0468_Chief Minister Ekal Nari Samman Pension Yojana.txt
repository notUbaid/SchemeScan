TITLE:
Chief Minister Ekal Nari Samman Pension Yojana

==================================================

BENEFITS:
- From 18 to 55 years old: Rs.
- 500/- per month.
- From 55 to 60 years: Rs.
- 750/- per month.
- From 60 to 75 years: 1000/- per month.
- Above the age of 75 years: 1500/- per month.

--------------------------------------------------

ELIGIBILITY:
- Rajasthan Chief Minister Ekal Nari Samman Pension Yojana Empowerment Financial Assistance Social Welfare The applicant's age should be 18 years or more.
- A native of the Rajasthan state.
- A widow/abandoned/divorced woman who does not have any source of regular income of her own for subsistence. (or)Annual income from all sources should be less than Rs.
- 48,000/-.
- Rajasthan Chief Minister Ekal Nari Samman Pension Yojana Empowerment Financial Assistance Social Welfare

--------------------------------------------------

DOCUMENTS:
- Aadhaar Card.
- Birth certificate.
- Ration Card.
- Original residence certificate.
- Bank Account

--------------------------------------------------

APPLICATION PROCESS:
Online The applicants have to visit on the official portal.On the home page, click on apply.On the new page, the application form will open.Now applicants need to enter the information asked in the application form.After entering all the information, upload the required documents.Click on the Submit.

--------------------------------------------------
