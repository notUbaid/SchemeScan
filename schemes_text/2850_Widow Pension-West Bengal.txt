TITLE:
Widow Pension-West Bengal

==================================================

BENEFITS:
- Financials Assistance

--------------------------------------------------

ELIGIBILITY:
- West Bengal Widow Pension-West Bengal Financial Assistance Pension Social Welfare Applicant must be widow girl/woman.
- The applicant must be a citizen of India and a resident of West Bengal.
- Her family income, if any, must not exceed ₹ 1,000/- per month.
- She must not be maintained by any of her relations.
- She must have been a resident of West Bengal for at least ten years on the date of making the application.
- Professional beggars and mendicants are not eligible for the pension.
- West Bengal Widow Pension-West Bengal Financial Assistance Pension Social Welfare

--------------------------------------------------

DOCUMENTS:
- Copy of Aadhaar self-attested.
- Copy of Ration Card.
- Copy of Voter Id.
- Copy of Disability Certificate.
- Copy of Income Certificate.
- Copy of Husband's Death Certificate.
- Copy of Bank Pass Book.
- Nomination Form (In case of death).
- Passport Size photograph.

--------------------------------------------------

APPLICATION PROCESS:
Offline Application Form(Form-P) for Pension may be obtained free of cost from following offices namely:Office of the Block Development Officer or the Executive Officer of the concerned Panchayat Samity in case of the applicant resides in the rural areas under the said Panchayat Samity.Office of the Sub-Divisional Officer in case of the applicant resides in Municipal / Notified areas outside the areas of Kolkata Municipal Corporation.Office of the Controller of Vagrancy, West Bengal situated at Purta Bhawan, Bidhan Nagar, Kolkata-700091, in case of the applicant resides within the areas under Kolkata Municipal Corporation.Required information for application form Person

--------------------------------------------------
