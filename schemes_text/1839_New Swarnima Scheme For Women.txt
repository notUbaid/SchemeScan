TITLE:
New Swarnima Scheme For Women

==================================================

BENEFITS:
- A subsidy amount of ₹2,00,000/- @ 5% per annum, for self-employment. (The remaining amount has to be self-owned by the beneficiary.)The beneficiary woman is not required to invest any amount of her own on the projects upto cost of Rs.
- 2,00,000/-.

--------------------------------------------------

ELIGIBILITY:
- Ministry Of Social Justice and Empowerment New Swarnima Scheme For Women ward Class Business Empowerment Entrepreneur Financial Assistance Women The applicant must be a FEMALE.
- The age of the applicant must be between 18 and 55 years.
- The applicant must be an Entrepreneur The Total Annual Family Income of the Applicant must be Less than ₹ 3 Lakh per Annum Ministry Of Social Justice and Empowerment New Swarnima Scheme For Women ward Class Business Empowerment Entrepreneur Financial Assistance Women

--------------------------------------------------

DOCUMENTS:
- Proof of Identity (Aadhaar card)Ration Card Domicile Certificate Caste Certificate (for reserved category)Passport size Photograph of the applicant

--------------------------------------------------

APPLICATION PROCESS:
Offline Step 1: Eligible applicant needs to visit nearest SCA office, apply on prescribed form for Swarnima Scheme for Women. You can find your nearest SCA office at this link - https://nsfdc.nic.in/channel-patrners/scas Step 2: Enter the necessary details in the application form and mention the needs and choice of vocation and training requirements, if any.Step 3: Submit your application form and the required documents to the same SCA office. After reviewing the application, the loan will be sanctioned by SCA.

--------------------------------------------------

DETAILS:
A term loan scheme by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment for women entrepreneurs from backward classes to obtain a loan of up to ₹2,00,000/- @ 5% per annum, thereby providing them social & financial security. The scheme is introduced by National ward Classes Finance and Development Corporation (NBCFDC) and implemented by State Channelising Agencies (SCAs) which act as the nodal agency.
