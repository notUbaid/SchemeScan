TITLE:
Chhatraon Ke Liye Protsahan Rashi

==================================================

BENEFITS:
- Senior Secondary Level:Girls studying agriculture in 11th and 12th standard will receive a scholarship of ₹15,000 per year.
- Undergraduate Level:Girls enrolled in undergraduate agricultural programs (4/5 years) like Horticulture, Dairy, Agricultural ineering, Food Processing, and Shri Karna Narendra Business School, Jobner will receive a scholarship of ₹25,000 per year.
- Postgraduate Level:Girls pursuing M.
- Agriculture (2 years) will receive a scholarship of ₹25,000 per year.
- Ph D Level:Girls pursuing Ph D in Agriculture (maximum 3 years) will receive a scholarship of ₹40,000 per year.
