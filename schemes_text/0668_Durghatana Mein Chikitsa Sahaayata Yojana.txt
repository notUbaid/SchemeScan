TITLE:
Durghatana Mein Chikitsa Sahaayata Yojana

==================================================

BENEFITS:
- To the registered beneficiaries, medical assistance of ₹ 20,000/- or the actual expenditure incurred on the treatment, whichever is less, will be provided.

--------------------------------------------------

ELIGIBILITY:
- Chhattisgarh Durghatana Mein Chikitsa Sahaayata Yojana Accident Financial Assistance Labor Workers The applicant must be a permanent resident of Chhattisgarh state.
- It is mandatory for construction workers to be registered in any district.
- Must have completed 90 days as a laborer in the board.
- Chhattisgarh Durghatana Mein Chikitsa Sahaayata Yojana Accident Financial Assistance Labor Workers

--------------------------------------------------

DOCUMENTS:
- Aadhar card.
- Permanent residence certificate.
- Bank account details.
- Hospital certificate.

--------------------------------------------------

APPLICATION PROCESS:
Offline Registered construction workers can apply through any CSC center or labor office.Get the application form.Fill out with required details.Attach the necessary documents.

--------------------------------------------------

DETAILS:
"Chhattisgarh Durghatana Mein Chikitsa Sahaayata Yojana" has been started by the Chhattisgarh Government and manage/run by the Chhattisgarh, Labor Department.
