TITLE:
Ladli Social Security Allowance Scheme

==================================================

BENEFITS:
- Under this scheme, a sum of ₹2750/- per month, per family will be given by the state government to those families who have solely girl child/children.
- The benefits will be provided for 15 years from the date of girls' parents completed 45 years of age.
- Note: After 60 years the "Ladli Social Security Allowance Scheme" will be converted into the "Old Age Samman Allowance Scheme".

--------------------------------------------------

ELIGIBILITY:
- Haryana Ladli Social Security Allowance Scheme Allowance Daughters Financial Assistance Girl Child Social Security Any family where biological single parent/parents are domicile of Haryana or working for the Government of Haryana and having no son, biological or adopted, but only daughter/daughters are eligible to get benefit.
- The gross annual income from all sources of the family must not exceed ₹2,00,000/- to be eligible for getting benefit under this scheme.
- The eligible family will be entitled to receive benefit under this scheme for a period of 15 years from the date either of the two parents completes 45 years of age.
- The benefit will be paid to the mother, if surviving.
- In case the mother is not surviving, the benefit will be paid to the father.

DETAILS:
The scheme "Ladli Social Security Allowance Scheme" was launched by the Department of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of Haryana on 1st January 2006. This scheme is on the pattern of "Old Age Allowance Scheme" for families having only a girl child/children is to be started for person having the age of 45 years and above. Under this scheme, a sum of ₹2750/- per month, per family will be given by the state government to those families who have solely girl child/children. The enrolment of families under this scheme commences from the 45th birthday of the mother or the father i.e. for 15 years. In case of the death of either of the parent the surviving parent will get this. Initially ₹300 p.m. per family was given. The Government has enhanced the rate of allowance from ₹300/- p.m. to ₹500/- p.m. and reduced the eligibility age from 55 years to 45 years w.e.f. 01.04.2007, to ₹1000/- p.m. w.e.f. 01.04.2014, to ₹1200/- p.m. w.e.f. 01-01-2015. The Government has increased the rates under the scheme to ₹1400/- p.m per beneficiary w.e.f 1-1-2016, ₹1600/- w.e.f. 01-11-2016, ₹1800/- w.e.f. 01-11-2017, ₹2000/- w.e.f. 01-11-2018, ₹2250/- w.e.f. 01.01.2020, ₹2500/- w.e.f. 01.04.2021 and ₹2750/- w.e.f. 01.04.2023.
