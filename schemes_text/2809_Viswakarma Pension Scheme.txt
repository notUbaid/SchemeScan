TITLE:
Viswakarma Pension Scheme

==================================================

BENEFITS:
- The monthly pension amount of ₹1100/- will be given to the persons belonging to the traditional Viswakarma Communities.

--------------------------------------------------

ELIGIBILITY:
- Kerala Viswakarma Pension Scheme Carpenters Goldsmiths Iron Worker Moosari Pension Stone Worker Viswakarma Community Wood Worker 1.
- The applicant should be a permanent resident of the Kerala State.
- The applicant should belong to the traditional Viswakarma Community of Kerala State.
- The age of the applicant should be more than 60 years.
- The annual family income of the applicant should be ₹1,00,000/-.
- Kerala Viswakarma Pension Scheme Carpenters Goldsmiths Iron Worker Moosari Pension Stone Worker Viswakarma Community Wood Worker

--------------------------------------------------

DOCUMENTS:
- Copy of the Identity proof2.
- Proof of age3.

- Residence proof8.
- Physical Disability Certificate, if applicable9.
- Copy of Bank Passbook10.
- Any other document, if required

--------------------------------------------------

APPLICATION PROCESS:
Offline Online Step 01: The applicant should visit the Official Website of the ward Classes Development Department, Government of Kerala, and download the application form.Step 02: Fill out the application form completely and send it along with all the relevant documents to the following addresses:Director, ward Classes Development Department Ayyankali Bhavan, 4th Floor, Kanakanagar Vellayambalam, Kawdiar P.O, Thiruvananthapuram- 695003, Kerala

--------------------------------------------------

DETAILS:
The Scheme "Viswakarma Pension" was launched by the ward Classes Development Department, Government of Kerala to give a pension to traditional workers of Viswakarma Communities (Carpenters (wood, iron, stone), Goldsmiths, Moosari) who have completed 60 years of age in the Kerala State.
