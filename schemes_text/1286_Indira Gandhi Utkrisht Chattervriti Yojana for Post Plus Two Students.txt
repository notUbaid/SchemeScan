TITLE:
Indira Gandhi Utkrisht Chattervriti Yojana for Post Plus Two Students

==================================================

BENEFITS:
- A scholarship of of ₹ 18,000/- per annum.

--------------------------------------------------

ELIGIBILITY:
- Himachal Pradesh Indira Gandhi Utkrisht Chattervriti Yojana for Post Plus Two Students Education Financial Assistance Scholarship Student 1.
- The applicant should be a resident of Himachal Pradesh.
- The applicant should have passed Class 12 in Science/Arts/Commerce.
- The applicant should have secured 60% or more marks in class 12th.
- The applicant should be a merit list holder from the Himachal Pradesh Board of School Education, Dharamshala.
- The applicant should have been admitted in an academic/professional course. requires residency in Himachal Pradesh, a minimum 60% marks in Class 12, and admission to an academic or professional course.
- What is the objective of the "Indira Gandhi Utkrisht Chattervriti Yojana for Post Plus Two Students" scholarship scheme?
- The scheme aims to provide a scholarship of ₹18,000 per annum to meritorious students pursuing academic or professional courses in Himachal Pradesh.
- Is there any application fee or cost associated with applying for this scheme?
- The entire application process is entirely free of cost.
- There are no application fees.
- Is It Mandatory to Have a Bank Account for This Scheme?
- Yes, possession of a valid bank account is a mandatory requirement for participation in this scheme.
- All scheme benefits are disbursed directly to the beneficiary's registered bank account.
- Can Applicants From The Neighboring States Also Apply To This Scheme?
- This scheme is exclusively available to permanent residents of the state.
- Applicants from neighboring states, among others, are not eligible to apply.

APPLICATION PROCESS:
Online Step 1: Go to the Official Website of the National Scholarship Portal. and click "New Registration". Guidelines for Registration will appear. Scroll to the bottom. Read carefully the undertaking. Accept the Terms. Click "Continue". Step 2: A Registration Form will appear. The fields marked as * are mandatory. Fill in the details and click "Register". Your Application ID and password will be displayed. The same will also be sent as an SMS to your registered mobile number. Step 3: Go to https://scholarships.gov.in/fresh/newstd Regfrm Instruction Click on "Login to Apply". Enter your Application ID and password. Type the Captcha and click "Login". On the next screen, provide the OTP received on your registered mobile number. You will be directed to the Password Reset screen. Create a new password and confirm. Click "Submit". You will be directed to the "Applicant's Dashboard". Step 4: On the left pane, click "Application Form". The fields marked as * are compulsory. Fill in the details and upload the documents. You can either click on "Save as Draft" to complete the application later. Else, click "Final Submit" to submit the application.NOTE: All the applicants are advised to fill the form correctly, no changes can be made once submitted. The applicants are also advised to fill in only one application as multiple applications will cancel the form.

--------------------------------------------------

DETAILS:
The scheme "Indira Gandhi Utkrisht Chattervriti Yojana for Post Plus Two Students" is a Scholarship Scheme by the Department of Education, Government of Himachal Pradesh. Through this scheme, a scholarship of ₹ 18,000/- per annum will be awarded to 10 meritorious students each from Class 12 (Arts, Science, and Commerce) and from the merit list of B.A., B.Sc. and B.Com. degree. The scholarship will be renewed yearly until they complete the degree/course.
