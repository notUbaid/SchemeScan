ELIGIBILITY:
- Odisha Banishree Scholarship Banishree Scholarship Education & Learning Scholarship Student Finance For the purpose of award of scholarship under the scheme, applications can be submitted subject to fulfilling the following conditions:-The applicant must be a bona fide resident of Orissa.
- The applicant should be a regular student of an educational institution.
- The parental/ family income of the applicant is not more than Rs.
- 60,000 per annum.
- The applicant is not receiving any financial assistance/stipend/scholarship from State Govt./ Central Govt. under any other scheme.
- The student will continue to get the scholarship if he enrols and regularly attends any recognised institution irrespective of whether he has passed or not in a particular year Odisha Banishree Scholarship Banishree Scholarship Education & Learning Scholarship Student Finance

DOCUMENTS:
- Documents to accompany the application Each application shall be accompanied by the following documents:-Disability Certificate issued by Medical Board Income Certificate issued by Revenue Officer, not below the rank of Tahasildar or any other officer of equivalent status.
- Mark sheets of the last examination passed duly attested by a Gazetted Officer or the Head of the Institution concerned.
- NOTE: An income Certificate is not required in case the student belongs to a BPL family.
