TITLE:
Vidhva Vivah Uphar Yojana

==================================================

BENEFITS:
- Financial Assistance/Gift amount ₹51,000/-

--------------------------------------------------

ELIGIBILITY:
- Rajasthan Vidhva Vivah Uphar Yojana Financial Assistance Marriage Widow Women Applicant must be a widow women from Rajasthan State.
- Applicant must be resident of Rajasthan or living in Rajasthan for 3 years before re- marriage.
- Applicant should be entitled to pension as per widow pension rules.
- Applicant age should be between 18-50 years.
- If the applicant has remarried before this scheme then he/she will not be eligible.
- Rajasthan Vidhva Vivah Uphar Yojana Financial Assistance Marriage Widow Women

--------------------------------------------------

DOCUMENTS:
- The applicant must have the following documents to apply for Widow Marriage Gift Scheme :-Aadhar Card Copy Address Proof Copy Bank Passbook Copy Birth Certificate Copy : For age proof Death Certificate Copy : of Husband Jan Aadhar/Bhamashah Card Copy Passport Size Photo

--------------------------------------------------

APPLICATION PROCESS:
Offline Applicant need to get the application form from:Officer of the District Social Justice and Empowerment Department.Download from the official document page no. 7.On plain paper. Submit to the Officer of the District Social Justice and Empowerment Department.Attach all the required documents Age proof (school certificate, birth certificate)Husband's death certificate Residence proof Income proof (all sources)

--------------------------------------------------

DETAILS:
Vidhwa Vivah Uphar Yojana is a government Under the Social Justice and Empowerment Department, scheme that assists in the process of marriage of widows. This scheme is for those widows who have decided to start a new life after the time spent with their former husbands. The government provides a certain amount to widows, which can be used in the marriage of widows.
