TITLE:
Leprosy Pension Scheme

==================================================

BENEFITS:
- The rate of grant per beneficiary per month will be Rs.
- 3000/- who became disabled due to Leprosy.

--------------------------------------------------

ELIGIBILITY:
- Uttar Pradesh Leprosy Pension Scheme Disability Empowerment Financial Assistance Social Welfare It is necessary to be a permanent resident of Uttar Pradesh.
- Disabled due to leprosy (with any percentage of disability).
- Persons with disabilities due to leprosy may belong to any age group.
- Must be below the poverty and should be under Rs.
- 56,460/- per annum for urban and Rs.
- 46,080/- per annum for rural areas.
- Are not taking advantage of other pension schemes.
- Uttar Pradesh Leprosy Pension Scheme Disability Empowerment Financial Assistance Social Welfare
